Project Description

During the pandemic, due to government control, people have to reduce their outing activities. For some people, the difference may not be big, but for more people, they don’t like the depression of staying at home and hope to go out and relax. Recent studies have also shown that this increased anxiety resulting from COVID-19 could be particularly prevalent among people with a history of psychiatric problems. Most people feel depressed, their freedom is restricted, and they rely heavily on relaxation and socializing, so we designed a software to help people alleviate this situation.

People have special needs in special times. This travel social application can bring a simulated travel experience to the user’s senses through the device and will not encounter the dangers encountered in real situations. This app supports online face pinching to provide a social image for themselves in the virtual world, and we also encourage users to design and share mods to add fun.
